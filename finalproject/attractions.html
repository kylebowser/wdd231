Xavage Adventure Park
If you’re ready to unleash your wild side, Xavage Park by Grupo Xcaret is where adventure meets nature. Opened in 2019, this 57‑hectare eco‑adventure park is designed for thrill‑seekers of all ages, offering six original activities that let you decide just how daring you want to be.

  • Whitewater Rapids: Experience the only man‑made rapids in Cancun and Riviera Maya — a heart‑pounding ride that feels like real river rafting.
  • Monster Trucks: Drive exclusive off‑road vehicles through 15 obstacles on the longest man‑made circuit in the world.
  • Ropes Course: Test your balance and courage on aerial challenges high above the jungle.
  • Jet Boats: Speed through canals with 360° turns and sudden stops for pure adrenaline.
  • Kayaking: Paddle calmly through scenic canals for a more relaxed adventure.
  • Zip‑line Flight: Soar horizontally across the park on the longest flight of its kind in Cancun.

Open: Monday to Saturday 9-5pm

Xplore Adventure Park
If you’re craving adventure beyond the beach, Xplor Park is Cancun’s ultimate playground for thrill‑seekers and nature lovers alike. Nestled in the lush jungle of the Riviera Maya, this eco‑adventure park invites you to explore above, below, and through the earth in unforgettable ways.

  • Zip‑line Paradise: Fly across the treetops on the tallest zip‑lines in the region, with panoramic views of the jungle and Caribbean horizon.
  • Amphibious Vehicles: Drive rugged 4x4s through caves, rivers, and jungle trails for a pulse‑pounding ride.
  • Underground Rivers: Swim or raft through crystal‑clear waters surrounded by stalactites and stalagmites.
  • Unique Activities: Try the hammock splash zip‑line or the Toboganxote waterslide — a 5‑in‑1 ride ending in a wave pool.
  • All‑Inclusive Comfort: Admission includes unlimited access to activities, plus a buffet lunch and non‑alcoholic drinks to recharge between adventures.

Open: Monday to Saturday 9-5pm
