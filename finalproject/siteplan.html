Site Name: Cancun Paradise Packages

A website for booking Cancun vacation packages.

Site Purpose:

This site will highlight the amazing draws of the lovely Cancun area for potential visitors. It will provide a brief history of the Rich Cancun culture and Ruins and it will also have a page of resorts, parks, and activities for tourists to enjoy.

Scenarios:

What is there to do in Cancun?

Where can I stay in Cancun?

What is the history of Cancun?

How do I book a package?

Color Scheme:

  • Primary Color: White
  • Top Color: Green
  • Bot Color: Red
  • Text Color: Black

These colors represent the Mexican flag. The Middle background of the Page will be white like the middle of the Mexican flag. The headers will be Green and the footers will be Red. The text will be black for easy reading. Though some text might be green or red for accent.

Typography:

The font that will be used for this site is 'Roboto'.
