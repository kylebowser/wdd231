Current Event

Step back in time and celebrate Valentine’s Day at Layton City’s Liberty Ball, a family-friendly evening inspired by America’s past and presented as part of America250 celebration with live music, dancing, dance lessons, refreshments, costume contest, and giveaways! Families are encouraged to participate in our costume contest by dressing in their favorite era of American history from colonial inspired looks to classic Americana and early 1900s elegance! The evening will also include a beginner-friendly partner dance lesson inspired by the era, making it easy and fun for all ages to participate. Come celebrate history, family, and fun at this unique Valentine’s tradition reimagined as the Liberty Ball.
